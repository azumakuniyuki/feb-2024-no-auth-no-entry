Gmail SMTP errors and codes Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.

You might see the error messages and codes on this page when send email to Gmail accounts.The messages and codes can help you troubleshoot and resolve email delivery problems.

Gmail appends one or both of these identifiers to all error messages:

  • gsmtp (Google SMTP) is added to all errors.
  • gcdp (Google Custom Domain Policies) is added to errors resulting from customized rules created by a Google Workspace administrator.

For example, 550 5.7.1 This message violates example.com email policy. - gcdp <sessionid> - gsmtp means that the error is a result of a custom rule created by the administrator.

For more information, visit About SMTP error messages.
